Raspberry Blackberry Smoothie

Description

A delicious smoothie!

This Raspberry Blackberry Smoothie is a golden combination! Healthy smoothies with bright flavors and colors are the best!

Ingredients

Original recipe yields 2 servings
  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 (6 ounce) container vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place banana, raspberries, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes into a blender.
  2. Blend untill smooth.
